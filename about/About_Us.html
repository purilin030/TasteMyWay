Welcome to TasteMyWay.
The ultimate guide for international visitors ready to embark on a culinary adventure through Malaysia. This is your passport to a world of sizzling woks, fragrant spices, and bold flavors waiting to be discovered on every street corner. Our mission is to help you go beyond the typical tourist path and truly immerse yourself in the heart of Malaysian culture—one delicious bite at a time.

Join the Adventure.
Travel, explore, eat, and drink—let the vibrant tapestry of Malaysia’s local food scene guide your way. We invite you to enjoy every moment, record every flavor, and help us build the definitive guide to the country's incredible street food.

About TasteMyWay

TasteMyWay is your gateway to exploring Malaysia's diverse culture, cuisine, and destinations. We share stories, travel tips, and local experiences to make your journey unforgettable.
